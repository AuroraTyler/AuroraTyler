The Religions of Miodrend

There are 4 major religions on the continent of Miodrend.

Each religion has it's own history and evolution of practice. Some are more widely practiced than others and some are practiced more strictly while other must be practiced in secret.

Ancestory

Followers of this religion pray to their ancestors for guidance and protection. They believe that after death you go to the ancestral plane for the next phase of existance where you can watch over your family and protect them. However, they also believe that if no one prays to them they will fade from existence.

Austrayon

Followers of this religion believe that all things in the world have a spirit, and they must be respected. They tend to be vegetarian or if they do eat meat, they use all parts of the animal.

Madrian

A polythiestic religion that believes in two main gods, a king and queen, and their 8 children. There are also several minor gods as well, such as the spouses of the 8 children. Each god has a specific element or aspect of life that they watch over.

  1. Madria
    • Queen and Goddess of creation
  2. Tastrafos
    • King and God of destruction
  3. Lanka
    • Deity of love
    • Gender fluid. Uses they/them pronouns. Prefers Lanki when in their female form and Lanko in their male form.
  4. Kulchel
    • The Goddess of water
    • pronounced KAH-shell or KUH-shell
  5. Cerafina
    • The Goddess of fire
  6. Caden
    • The God of earth
    • His wife Fiorella is the goddess of flowers
  7. Zafir
    • The God of the sky
    • The sun and moon gods are part of his entourage
  8. Tiazi
    • The Goddess of death
    • Pronounced TEE-AH-ZEE
    • Her husband Emerais is in charge of the psychopomps
  9. Inara
    • The Goddess of intellect
    • Alara is her twin
  10. Alara
    • The Goddess of the physical
    • Inara is her twin
    • She is pansexual and polyamorous
  11. Astel
    • The God of communication and understanding
    • He developed the common tongue, Astellan, and gave it to the humans

Tallus

A monotheistic religion that worships a god named, Tallus.

In the beginning, there was Tallus, and there was the Emptiness. Tallus knew that the Emptiness could not be sustained, so she opened her arms wide, and from her the planets and stars filled the Emptiness with life and light.
