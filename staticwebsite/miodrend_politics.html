The Countries of Miodrend

There are 9 countries on the continent of Miodrend. Each has it's own government, customs and history. But the landscape is always breath taking.


Albon

Back to top

The government of Albon is a monarchy that is advised by a council. The advisors on the council are selected based on merit and service. It is a voluntary position, but typically life long.
The main religion of the country is Tallus.

Austrayo

Back to top

Austrayo has an absolute monarchy that focuses on nurturing the arts.
The landscape is filled with rivers, lakes and forests with many marshlands and swamps. There is an island in the middle of one of their larger lakes that is considered magical and sacred.
The main religion is Austrayon.

Contra

Back to top

The capitol of Contra is Hylara. It was originally called The City of Her Highness Alara, but was eventually shortened.
The government is a monarchy led by a council of nobles that advise the sovereign.
The largest lake, Hyluria, is shared with Luria and contains a kingdom of Merfolk and a giant squid.
They are a close ally of Syrasa.
The official religion of Contra is Madrian, although it's practice is not enforced, nor other religious practices persecuted.

Elladia

Back to top

The government of Elladia is a parlimentary monarchy.
Their major religion is Madrian.

Esorni

Back to top

Esorni is a military dictatorship that is mostly benevolent. Their monarchy was deposed when the mining conditions in the country necessitated more firm ruling.
The royal family is currently in hiding.
The country is rich from mining.
The official religion is Ancestory and is strictly enfored.

Luria

Back to top

The Lurian government is an absolute monarchy that is in constant conflict with Mezerhan and Esorni over a stretch of land near the coast.
It shares Lake Hyluria with Contra and the Angshire Peninsula with Mezerhan.
Due to a shared history with Mezerhan, they are strong allies.
There is no official religion, and all four main religions on the continent are practiced throughout the kingdom.

Mezerhan

Back to top

Mezerhan is an absolute monarchy with strong ties to the kingdom of Luria.
It shares the Angshire Peninsula with Luria.
Throughout the kingdom, all of the major religions are practiced with the exception of Madrian.

Portuz

Back to top

The Empire of Portuz is best known for their extreme hospitality.
Tallus is practiced almost exclusively.

Syrasa

Back to top

The monarchy of Syrasa is head by a grand council.
Despite it's geographical closeness to Luria and Elldia, it's main ally is Contra.
There is no official religion, but Tallus and Madrian are the main religions practiced throughout the kingdom.
